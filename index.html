Why buy real estate in Bulgaria? 

Bulgaria is an amazing country that combines rich historical and cultural heritage, unique nature, mild climate and hospitable people. Bulgaria offers many opportunities for living, vacationing, investing and doing business. Here are some of the advantages of buying real estate in Bulgaria:
- Low real estate prices compared to other European countries, as well as the prospect of rising prices in the future due to Bulgaria's accession to the Eurozone and Schengen.
- Favorable conditions for foreigners to buy real estate in Bulgaria, such as no restrictions on the area and number of objects, the possibility of obtaining residency and citizenship, low taxes and fees.
- Variety of real estate in Bulgaria, which will satisfy all preferences and goals. You can buy real estate on the Black Sea coast, in ski resorts or health resorts, in historical or modern cities, in picturesque villages or on lakes and rivers. - High quality of life in Bulgaria, which is provided by favorable climate, ecology, health care, education, culture, sports and entertainment. Bulgaria is also a safe and stable country with friendly people and Slavic mentality.
